Sustainable bike transport for all

We’re building a future where cycling is even more environmentally friendly. Will you join us?

Bamboo is an ideal material for a bike. It is sturdy, flexible, and, unlike other materials, it doesn’t require intensive processing. Basically, it’s the natural choice.

With bamboo at the heart of what we do, we are designing three models of frame available in various sizes for the environmentally-minded cyclist. We will also be able to offer customisation and will tailor the frame to your measurements, adapting the model so it is uniquely designed for your comfort. Further customisation will be available with different colour and staining options should you desire it. We aim to cater to your needs as a cyclist.
